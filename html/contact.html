About Sweden's First Korean Steakhouse


KOMO represents Chef Orkhon's 25-year journey with Korean cuisine. Blending his Mongolian heritage and deep understanding of meat culture with his passion for Korean culinary traditions, he created Sweden's first Korean steakhouse.
